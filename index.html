The Phoenix Suns Basketball Team

The Phoenix Suns are an American professional basketball team based in Phoenix, Arizona. They compete in the National
Basketball Association (NBA), as a member of the league's Western Conference Pacific Division. The Suns are the only
team in their division not to be based in California, and play their home games at the Footprint Center.
